About Publications

Iksung KANG

I am a computational optical imaging scientist at the intersection of optical imaging and machine learning.

I focus on developing intelligent imaging systems that seamlessly integrate physical principles, computation, and system-level design, to make advanced imaging technologies more accessible, scalable, and reliable.

Previously, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow at UC Berkeley, advised by Professor Na Ji and Stella X. Yu, and a PhD from MIT EECS. At MIT, I was advised by Professor George Barbastathis and was a recipient of Korea Foundation for Advanced Studies scholarship throughout my doctoral study.
